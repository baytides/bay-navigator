# Eligibility Guides

Many discount programs require proof of eligibility. These guides explain who qualifies for each category, how to enroll or verify your status, and where to apply in person across the nine Bay Area counties.

**Note:** Eligibility requirements and enrollment processes can change. Always verify current information with the official program websites linked in each guide.

---

## Choose a Guide

<div class="eligibility-grid" markdown="1">

### [Public Assistance Programs]({{ site.baseurl }}/eligibility/public-assistance.html)
Learn about Medi-Cal, CalFresh (EBT/SNAP), and CalWORKs. Find out if you qualify, how to apply online or in person, and locate your county office.

### [Military, Veterans & Families]({{ site.baseurl }}/eligibility/military-veterans.html)
Understand who qualifies as active duty, veteran, National Guard, Reserve, or eligible family member. Learn about verification methods and ID requirements.

### [Students]({{ site.baseurl }}/eligibility/students.html)
Find out what counts as valid student status for discount programs, including enrollment verification, .edu email requirements, and student ID guidelines.

### [Seniors]({{ site.baseurl }}/eligibility/seniors.html)
Learn about age thresholds for senior discounts (which vary by program), how to verify your age, and senior-specific benefit programs.

### [People with Disabilities]({{ site.baseurl }}/eligibility/disability.html)
Understand eligibility for disability-related discounts, including SSI/SSDI recipients, Regional Center clients, and ADA accommodations.

### [Low-Income Utility Programs]({{ site.baseurl }}/eligibility/utility-programs.html)
Learn about CARE, FERA, and LIHEAP programs that reduce utility bills for qualifying households.
